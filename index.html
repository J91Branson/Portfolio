About Me:





I started learning code and then enrolled in a Bootcamp at SMU for Web-Development, during my time at that bootcamp I really enjoyed the design aspect of building websites.

So I enrolled in a certification program for Graphic design at SMU, working on that currently.

Towards the end of camp I realized I would need to build a portfolio and to achieve this it would be easier if I built a excellent team, so I reached out to a few friends and established Gorilla Syndicate LLC. Together we handle all kinds of thing focusing on Web-Development, we also handle Website, and Graphic Design.

During this side project I have gained project management skills and we have all practiced our teamwork and development skills.

A couple of other cool thing I have had a chance to be apart of is Icode Magazine and Coders In Hoods, I write articles and make presentations for these groups as well as give monthly conferences.
